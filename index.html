To those who haven't played any of these ARPG's before, here is my opinion on the pros and cons in case you're looking for a particular gaming experience!

Last Epoch

  • Pros
  • Intermediate learning curve
  • Lots of build variety
  • Stunning visuals
  • Very smooth gameplay
  • Cons
  • Unfinished game (beta)
  • Endgame is lacking

Diablo 3

  • Pros
  • Easy learning curve
  • Great Story
  • Frequently goes on sale
  • Can get to endgame content quickly
  • Cons
  • Rarely gets fresh/new content
  • Endgame can get grindy, even for an ARPG

Grim Dawn

  • Pros
  • Intermediate learning curve
  • Lots of different builds and items
  • Has gauntlet game mode, refreshing in ARPG genre
  • Has modding support
  • Cons
  • No longer receives updates
  • Placeholder until I can think go another con

Path of Exile

  • Pros
  • Lots of freedom in build choice
  • Endgame has boatloads of content
  • Receives fresh and exciting updates quarter-anuualy
  • Cons
  • Difficult learning curve
  • Rerolling a character is needlessly expensive
  • Build diversity is at the mercy of the developers
